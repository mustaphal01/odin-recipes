Fried Rice

This chicken fried rice tastes just like your favorite hibachi restaurant! That’s because we took this recipe straight from the hibachi grill and our mega-popular Hibachi Chicken recipe. On the table in just about 20 minutes, this fried rice is perfect as a main course or a side dish, and it’s guaranteed to please the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 egs, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the frozen peas and carrots and cook for a few minutes until they're heated through.
  4. Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until they're cooked through.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir everything together.
  6. Add the soy sauce and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
